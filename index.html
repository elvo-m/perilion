Our Services

Image Annotation Services

Power your AI/ML initiatives with pixel-perfect image annotation. Our experts label visual data using bounding boxes, polygon segmentation, and keypoint markers for object detection, facial recognition, and autonomous systems. With rigorous QA protocols, we deliver training data that enhances model reliability. Trust our secure industry-specific solutions to turn raw images into actionable AI insights.

Text Annotation Services

Unlock NLP potential with precise text annotation. We classify, tag, and label textual data for sentiment analysis, entity recognition, and intent detection, empowering chatbots, search engines, and customer analytics tools. Our linguists annotate multilingual datasets, capturing context, syntax, and semantics to refine language models. Partner with us for scalable, GDPR-compliant text labeling that drives smarter, faster AI decision-making.

Video Annotation Services

Train dynamic AI models with frame-by-frame video annotation. We track objects, annotate activities, and label events in real-time footage for surveillance, sports analytics, and autonomous robotics. Using advanced tools, we identify motion patterns, classify behaviors, and segment scenes to enhance model adaptability. Scale your video-based AI applications with data that’s meticulously labeled for accuracy, speed, and real-world complexity.

3D Point Cloud Annotation

Fuel LiDAR and autonomous systems with precise 3D annotation. We annotate point cloud data for depth perception, object tracking, and spatial mapping, critical for self-driving vehicles, drones, and robotics. Using cuboid labeling, semantic segmentation, and sensor fusion, we deliver rich 3D datasets that mimic real-world environments. Rely on our expertise to build robust, safety-critical AI models with data that captures every dimension.
